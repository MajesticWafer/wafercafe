The Permian Period: A Turning Point in Earth’s History

Essential Question

What were the key events and changes during the Permian period, and how did they shape the future of life on Earth?

Claim

The Permian period was a time of significant geological and biological changes, culminating in a mass extinction that reshaped life on Earth.

Key Details

Formation of Pangaea

Unified landmass led to diverse climates and altered habitats.

Climate Shifts

Transition from glaciation to warmer, drier conditions.

Evolution of Reptiles

Rise of therapsids, ancestors of mammals.

Permian Mass Extinction

Largest extinction, eliminating 90-96% of marine species.

Permian-Triassic Boundary

Set the stage for the Mesozoic Era and the rise of dinosaurs.
