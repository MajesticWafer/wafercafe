Privacy Policy

Welcome to The Wafer Café! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, and any other details you share when contacting us. Additionally, we collect certain data automatically through cookies and other technologies when you visit our site, including but not limited to IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior.

Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you interact with our site. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Google Advertisements

We use Google Ads to display advertisements on our site. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites. The data collected by Google is used to personalize the ads you see.

Google AdSense and Analytics

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s previous visits to this website. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings page.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate and maintain our website, improve user experience, and for advertising purposes. We may also use your information to respond to your inquiries or communicate with you about updates.

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also opt out of cookies or personalized ads as described above. If you have any concerns regarding your data, please contact us via [GitHub issues](https://github.com/MajesticWafer/wafercafe/issues).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
